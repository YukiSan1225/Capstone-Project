About ERPG


In a fast paced world where techonology continues to advance,the need for privacy and security has increased substaintally. Weak and reused passwords play a major role in most hacking-related breaches. There are many tips and suggestions for creating better passwords, but people often use personal information to create passwords or recycle commonly used passwords. To avoid breaches and leaks of confidential information, strong and unique passwords have to be used. Furthermore, safety and convenience should be offered as one.


ERPG is a mobile and desktop friendly application the helps users create, store, and maintain their passwords. Each password will be unique and contain at least 16 characters varying between numbers, uppercase/lowercase letters, and special symbols. Users will be able to visit the website and generate a new password with the click of a button. Additionally, users can create an account, which gives them permission to store all the passwords in a secure area. It also links certain passwords to certain websites. This is a convenient way for users to recall their password and avoid having to reset it.
